Two Ways to Create Art

Choose your path.

Not sure which track is right for you? You can always start with one and switch, or try a bit of both. Milky will help you figure it out.

1
Track One

Core Technique Track

For students who want to actually learn to draw and paint, not just dabble. This track builds real, measurable skills through structured, personalized instruction.

  • Real skills, real progress
    You'll see yourself improve, week by week.
  • Strong fundamentals
    Drawing first, then painting when you're ready.
  • Personalized curriculum
    Built around your skill level, not a generic syllabus.
  • Homework between sessions
    Keeps momentum building and skills sharp.
  • Results in weeks, not years
    A focused curriculum that moves at your pace.
Start the Core Track →
2
Track Two

Intuitive Flow Track

For students who want art as a creative outlet, a mindful practice, or a way to express what words can't. No pressure. No grades. Just you and what comes out.

  • Mixed media exploration
    Paint, collage, texture, whatever feels right.
  • Express your emotions
    Art as a way to process and connect with yourself.
  • Mindful, pressure free
    No rigid rules. No right answers.
  • Art as practice, not performance
    It's about the process, not just the output.
  • Exploration and joy
    Something you genuinely look forward to.
